🚀 More Shortcuts for Chrome

Add more shortcuts to Chrome to improve your browsing experience.

⌨️ Shortcuts
════════════

🔎 Search selection in a new tab
   Shortcut: Ctrl+E
   Why: Quickly search the selected text without interrupting your workflow.

📑 Open a new tab to the right
   Shortcut: Ctrl+T
   Why: Chrome normally opens new tabs at the end of the tab strip, separating related tabs. This shortcut keeps new tabs right next to the current one.
